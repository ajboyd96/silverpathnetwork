1. Information We Collect

Silver Path Network ("we," "us," or "our") collects the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, email address, phone number, mailing address, and ZIP code
  • Health Information: Tobacco use status, general health condition information
  • Insurance Information: Coverage amount preferences, previous insurance history
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with insurance quotes and recommendations
  • To connect you with licensed insurance agents and companies
  • To communicate with you about our services

  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
