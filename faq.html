General Questions

Final expense insurance is a type of whole life insurance designed to cover end-of-life costs such as funeral expenses, medical bills, and outstanding debts. It provides peace of mind by ensuring your family won't face financial hardship during an already difficult time.

Premiums start as low as $15 per month and vary based on your age, health, coverage amount, and tobacco use. Most people find it surprisingly affordable, especially compared to the potential costs it covers.

No medical exam is required for most final expense policies. You'll only need to answer a few simple health questions. This makes it accessible even if you have pre-existing health conditions.

Coverage & Benefits

Most people choose between $5,000 and $25,000 in coverage. Consider funeral costs ($7,000-$15,000), outstanding medical bills, credit card debt, and any final expenses you want covered. Our quiz helps determine the right amount for your situation.

Final expense insurance provides a cash benefit that can be used for any purpose, including funeral and burial costs, outstanding medical bills, credit card debt, mortgage payments, or any other final expenses your family may face.

Most policies have immediate coverage for accidental death. For natural death, some policies have a 2-year waiting period, while others offer immediate full coverage. We work with carriers that offer immediate coverage options.

Application Process

Most applications are approved within 24-48 hours. Some carriers offer instant approval for qualified applicants. The simple application process means you can get covered quickly.

Final expense insurance has very lenient underwriting. Most people qualify regardless of health conditions. Even if you have serious health issues, guaranteed issue policies are available that accept all applicants.

You'll need basic personal information, your Social Security number, and answers to a few simple health questions. The entire application takes just a few minutes to complete.
